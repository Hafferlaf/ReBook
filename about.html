About ReBook

Project ReBook is a project that tries to make it easier for students to trade their books when they are no longer using them. We are developing ReBook in collaboration with a Student Cafe on ITU that wants to deliver this service to their fellow students. In most cases students, that are further in their study, will sell their books to other students that is yet to complete their common courses. This means that there is already a trend for such activity and our application will try to accommodate the demand by making this a platform available specifically for such exchange.
